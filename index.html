Welcome to STAAR. The Software Disaster Research Center aims to establish a public and national response system to SW disasters controlled by software such as automobiles, aviation, medical care, and finance, and will develop the world's most accurate SW error diagnosis technology, SW error correction technology, and SW disaster management system.
